Types of Crypto Wallets
Online wallets are stored in the cloud. It‚Äôs a service used to store your Public Account and Private Keys. It can be accessed any where and any time. The cloud service protects your keys, with backups and security to prevent viruses, key loggers, losing passwords. Risks include you must request the service to perform your transactions. Also must ensure they will not lose your keys. It has happened!
Desktop Wallet is an application you store on your computer. Normally very secure, but the responsibility to secure it and possibility of losing it is up to the owner. Risks include backups, viruses, key loggers, and losing passwords.
Mobile wallets are stored on smart phones and tables. It‚Äôs an application. Can be used any where and any time. Can also scan QR codes for payments. Risks include losing the device, backups, viruses, key loggers, losing passwords. Can use finger print as keys.
A paper wallet is just a document with your Public Account Name and Private Keys. Risks: Is secure from hacking but the document can be lost or stolen. Data must be entered manually to perform a transaction.
